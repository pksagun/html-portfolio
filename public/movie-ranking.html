The Best Movies According to Sagun

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Titanic

I loved the movie. The love between Jack & Rose has been beautifully presented.

3 Idiots

This is a best bollywood movie I have ever watched. It is based on a story of three boys who met in an engineering college while pursuing their degree.

A motivational movie by Tom Hanks. Really inspiring.
